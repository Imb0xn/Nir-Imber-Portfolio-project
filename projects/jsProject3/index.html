Welcome to Element Builder!

Quick Guide:
1. Choose an Element: Pick a type (like H1, Paragraph) from the dropdown.
2. Customize: Type your text, set colors, size, spacing, and more in the fields.
3. Create: Press "Create Element" to see your design on the right.
4. Manage: Use "Delete Board" to clear the screen, or "Save to Local Storage" to keep your layout.

Enjoy crafting your unique page elements!
